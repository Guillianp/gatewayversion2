Pastelitos

Pastelitos (empanadas) are deep fried patties made of highly seasoned ground beef, pork, chicken, shrimp, or spinach and cheese stuffed with chopped olives, and sometimes with bits of egg and raisins. In the Dominican Republic you can get a one empanada for less than $0.35 USD

Yaroa

Yaroa is a Dominican street food that originated from Santiago. It is made from French fries or a mash of a root vegetable or tuber such as plantain or yuca, then layered with meat and then cheese. Condiments such as mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard are also often added on top. In the Dominican Republic you can purchase a serving of Yaroa for roughly $6.25 USD

Dominican Chimi

Chimi (short for chimichurri) is the Dominican hamburger, which shares a name with the famous Argentinian chimichurri sauce. The difference betweenthe the Chimichurri burger from other burgers. One is the bread (generally Pan de Agua). The other is using shredded cabbage instead of lettuce. And it never includes cheese. The price of a chimi is roughfly $1.50USD.
