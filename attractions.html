ATV through the countryside

As you embark on an inland safari, get behind the wheel of an ATV or a quad. From Puerto Plata to Punta Cana, tours are available that take riders through unspoiled nature and local villages. The trip is an excellent opportunity to travel off the beaten path and meet locals along the way. The average cost for a full day is around $139USD per person.This activity is only for persons with a valid license.

Snorkeling

Visit one of the most beautiful islands on the North Coast. Paradise Island will welcome you with turquoise water, white sand and colorful reef. Visit the Monte Cristi National Park where you can explore the mangrove jungle and see local wild life.Go ashore to sunbathe and explore or take a guided snorkeling tour during which you will see a variety of tropical fish and coral. The cost per person is of approximately $89.00USD. This activity can be done by everyone.

Santo Domingo Tour

Visit the oldest city in the New World and the first permanent settlement of Christopher Columbus after his "discovery" of the Americas. This incredible historic area boasts some of the oldest buildings in the New World. Top attractions include the Catedral Primada de América, the first cathedral in America, and the Alcazar de Colón, the former residence of Christopher Columbus's son.The tour cost is of $79.00USD. This activity can be enjoyed by the whole family.
