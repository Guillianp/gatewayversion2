DOMINICAN REPUBLIC HAS IT ALL

The Dominican Republic is the Caribbean's second largest and most diverse country, located just two hours south of Miami, less than four hours from New York, and eight hours from most European cities. The Dominican Republic, known for its warm and welcoming people, is a destination unlike any other, with breathtaking natural beauty, intriguing history, and a rich culture.
